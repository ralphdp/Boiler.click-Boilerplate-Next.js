# Boiler Privacy Protocol & Sovereignty
 
## I. Data Sovereignty
The Boiler environment operates on the principle of **Total Localism**. Your code, your reasoning logs, and your architectural blueprints are your property. 

## II. Information Handling
Traditional IDEs and "AI Copilots" harvest developer intent to train centralized models. Boiler rejects this extractive paradigm.

### 1. Local Residency
All telemetry and reasoning logs generated within Boiler are stored locally on your machine. We do not transmit your source code or sensitive logic to third-party silos.

### 2. Verified Handshakes
When Boiler interacts with the cloud (e.g., for collaboration or updates), it does so through verified, minimal handshakes. Your identity is cryptographically shielded, and your work remains within your sovereign boundary.

## III. Your Rights
As a Sovereign Architect using Boiler, you have the absolute right to:
- Audit any telemetry generated by the system.
- Reclaim (delete) all local data at your discretion.
- Operate in "Air-Gap" mode with zero external connectivity.
